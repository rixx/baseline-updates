The `e`, `pi`, `infinity`, and `NaN` keywords represent well-defined constants accepted in CSS math functions such as `calc()`.

- ``css.types.calc-keyword.e`` [[mdn]](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/search?q=css.types.calc-keyword.e)
- ``css.types.calc-keyword.NaN`` [[mdn]](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/search?q=css.types.calc-keyword.NaN)
- ``css.types.calc-keyword.infinity`` [[mdn]](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/search?q=css.types.calc-keyword.infinity)
