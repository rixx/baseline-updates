Zstandard or `zstd` is fast lossless compression algorithm. When used as a content encoding, it is often faster and offers better compression than brotli.

- ``http.headers.Accept-Encoding.zstd`` [[mdn]](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/search?q=http.headers.Accept-Encoding.zstd)
- ``http.headers.Content-Encoding.zstd`` [[mdn]](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/search?q=http.headers.Content-Encoding.zstd)
