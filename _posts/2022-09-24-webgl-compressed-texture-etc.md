[caniuse](https://caniuse.com/?search=webgl-compressed-texture-etc) · [mdn](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/search?q=WEBGL_compressed_texture_etc WebGL extension) · [spec](https://registry.khronos.org/webgl/extensions/WEBGL_compressed_texture_etc/)

The `WEBGL_compressed_texture_etc` extension for WebGL 1.0 and 2.0 contexts adds many ETC2 and EAC compressed texture formats to `compressedTexImage2D()` and `compressedTexSubImage2D()`.
